Mapping Black Philly Art is a digital humanities project that uses Wikidata, SPARQL, and Python to document and visualize information about Black artists in Philadelphia. The project is a collaboration between the Philadelphia Museum of Art and the Loretta C. Duckworth Scholars Studio at Temple University Libraries.
